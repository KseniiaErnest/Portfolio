Hi, I am Kseniia

I am a trilingual enthusiast and web developer with a passion for connecting cultures and creating captivating digital experiences. Here's a bit more about my journey:

My Background

Originally born and raised in the beautiful city of Saint-Petersburg, Russia, I developed an early fascination with languages and cultures. This interest led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Asian and African Studies, specializing in Japan and the Japanese language. During my academic journey, I immersed myself in the rich culture and language of Japan, fostering a deep appreciation for its traditions and modern innovations.

Tokyo: A Second Home

The pursuit of my passion led me to Tokyo, Japan, where I resided for 6.5 enriching years. Embracing Tokyo's vibrant lifestyle and immersing myself in the local culture and language became an unforgettable experience. During my time in Japan, I not only sharpened my language skills but also cultivated a deep appreciation for the fusion of tradition and modernity.

Empowering Web Development

Recognizing the power of technology to bring people together, I made the leap into web development. Currently, I'm actively engaged in a comprehensive web development boot camp, honing my skills as a full-stack MERN developer. Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, React, and MongoDB, I'm enthusiastic about creating seamless and innovative web applications.

With my trilingual abilities in English, Japanese, and Russian, I endeavor to dissolve language barriers and build user-centric web experiences that resonate with international audiences. By blending cultural insights and cutting-edge technology, I aim to foster meaningful connections and create inclusive digital solutions.

Beyond code, I believe in the strength of collaboration. I eagerly seek out like-minded individuals and organizations to embark on projects that foster cross-cultural understanding and make a positive impact.
